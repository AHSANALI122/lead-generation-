# Product knowledge

## What the product is
We are a cloud-based customer engagement platform that helps teams capture, qualify,
and follow up with leads automatically. It combines a conversational assistant, a
shared inbox, and analytics in one place.

## Pricing plans
We offer three plans: Starter at $29/seat/month, Pro at $79/seat/month, and
Enterprise with custom pricing. All plans are billed monthly or annually, and annual
billing saves about two months per year.

## Free trial
Every plan includes a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get full
access to Pro features during the trial so you can evaluate the product end to end.

## Integrations
We integrate with Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Gmail, Microsoft 365, and Zapier.
Salesforce and HubSpot sync leads two-way in near real time; other tools are available
through our Zapier app and a documented REST API.

## Security and compliance
The platform is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Data is encrypted in
transit with TLS and at rest with AES-256, and we support single sign-on (SSO) on the
Pro and Enterprise plans.

## Data ownership and export
Your data is always yours. You can export all leads and conversations to CSV at any
time, and the REST API lets you pull data programmatically without restrictions.

## Onboarding and setup
Most teams are live within a day. Starter and Pro are self-serve with guided setup,
while Enterprise customers get a dedicated onboarding specialist and a migration plan.

## Support
Starter includes email support with a one-business-day response target. Pro adds chat
support, and Enterprise includes a 24/7 priority line with a four-hour response SLA.

## Service level agreement
We guarantee 99.9% uptime on the Pro and Enterprise plans, backed by a financially
credited SLA. Live status and incident history are published on our public status page.

## Implementation and migration
We provide importers for CSV files and direct migrations from common CRMs. Enterprise
plans include hands-on migration support from our team at no extra cost.

## Cancellation and refunds
You can cancel anytime from billing settings; your plan stays active until the end of
the current billing period. Annual plans are eligible for a prorated refund within the
first 30 days.

## Custom limits and scale
Starter supports up to 1,000 contacts, Pro up to 50,000, and Enterprise is unlimited.
Higher API rate limits and dedicated infrastructure are available on Enterprise.
